Goals & Results

The goal is for students to be agents of their own education - capable of seeking independent scholarship to overcome obstacles, design their own solutions and not depend on others to stucture their education. Leaving the K12 environment, students should be the ones who guarantee the excellence of their education.

Our Mission

Teaching students to be agents of their own education, to seek independent scholarship and to build their own solutions.

Equity in education is a problem.

At first glance, it looks like we need to help more kids get to college.

Why we started?

All students have the capacity to drive their own education in such a way as to provide themselves with expanded life opportunities.All students have the capacity to learn to seek scholarship to overcome obstacles in the context of designing meaningful impact for their community. Where you grow up should not determine the opportunities you have in life.
